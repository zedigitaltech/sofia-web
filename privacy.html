Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Z.E Digital Tech ("we", "us", "our") operates Sofia AI ("Sofia", "the Service"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect personal data in connection with the Service, in compliance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable data protection laws.

1. What Data We Collect

1.1 Customer Data (Account Holders)

When you sign up for Sofia, we collect:

  • Business name and contact information (name, email, phone number)
  • Billing information (payment method details)
  • Business location information (name, address, phone numbers)
  • Knowledge base content you upload (services, pricing, policies, etc.)
  • Account preferences and configuration settings

1.2 Caller Data

When callers interact with Sofia, we process:

  • Caller phone number (from your telephony provider)
  • Voice audio streams (processed in real-time, not stored by default)
  • Speech-to-text transcriptions of conversations
  • Call metadata (timestamp, duration, language detected)
  • Lead qualification data extracted from conversations
  • Call recordings (only when explicitly enabled by the business)

1.3 Website Visitors

When you visit our website, we may collect:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages visited and interaction data
  • Referral source

2. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery: Processing phone calls, generating AI responses, qualifying bookings, and providing analytics.
  • Account management: Managing your subscription, processing payments, and communicating about service updates.
  • Service improvement: Analyzing aggregate usage patterns to improve Sofia's AI capabilities, voice quality, and response accuracy.
  • Support: Responding to your inquiries and providing technical assistance.
  • Legal compliance: Fulfilling legal obligations and enforcing our Terms of Service.

We do not sell personal data to third parties. We do not use caller data for advertising purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

Sofia uses the following third-party services to deliver the Service. Each processes data in accordance with their own privacy policies and GDPR compliance frameworks:

  • Zadarma (zadarma.com) — Telephony and SIP trunking. Processes caller phone numbers and routes calls. GDPR compliant infrastructure.
  • LiveKit (livekit.io) — Real-time audio routing. Processes voice data streams during active calls. Data is not stored.
  • Google Gemini (ai.google.dev) — Speech-to-speech AI processing. Processes voice audio to generate spoken responses. Data is processed under Google's GDPR DPA.
  • Hetzner (hetzner.com) — Application hosting. Hosts Sofia's infrastructure in EU data centres. GDPR compliant with DPA available.

4. Data Retention

We retain data for the following periods:

  • Account data: Retained for the duration of your subscription plus 90 days after termination.
  • Call transcriptions: Retained for 90 days by default, configurable per your preferences.
  • Call recordings: Retained for the period configured by the business (30, 60, or 90 days), then automatically deleted.
  • Analytics data: Aggregated analytics data is retained for 12 months. Individual call data is anonymized after the retention period.
  • Billing records: Retained for 7 years as required by tax and accounting regulations.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the GDPR, you and the data subjects whose data we process on your behalf have the following rights:

  • Right of access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you or your business's callers.
  • Right to rectification: You may request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to erasure: You may request deletion of personal data when it is no longer necessary for the purposes for which it was collected.
  • Right to data portability: You may request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to restrict processing: You may request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Right to object: You may object to our processing of your data for certain purposes.
  • Right to withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at elvizekaj@zedigital.tech. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data, including:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Secure WebSocket connections for voice data streams
  • Access controls and authentication for all systems
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Incident response procedures and breach notification protocols

7. International Data Transfers

Sofia's primary infrastructure is located in the European Union (Hetzner EU data centres). Some third-party services may process data in locations outside the EU. In such cases, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or adequacy decisions.

8. Cookie Policy

Our website does not use cookies. Authentication state for the client portal is kept in browser memory only and is cleared when you close the tab. We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email at least 30 days before they take effect. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised.
