Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Sofia AI, organized by topic.

General

Sofia is an AI-powered voice receptionist designed for businesses across every industry. She answers your phone calls 24/7 in natural language, handles inquiries about your services and offerings, qualifies leads, and transfers complex requests to your team with full context.

When someone calls your business, the call routes through a SIP trunk to Sofia's infrastructure. Google Gemini Live processes the audio end-to-end in real time — understanding the caller's speech and generating a natural voice response in one step, with no separate transcription or text-to-speech pipeline. Typical response latency is under 500 milliseconds.

Sofia currently supports Albanian, English, Italian, German, French, and Spanish — six languages total. Sofia auto-detects the caller's language and responds naturally without any configuration needed. The Starter plan includes 2 languages. Professional and Enterprise plans include all six languages. Additional languages can be added upon request for Enterprise customers.

Sofia uses state-of-the-art text-to-speech technology that produces very natural-sounding voice output with proper intonation and pacing. While we comply with GDPR requirements to inform callers about AI processing, many callers report that the experience feels very much like talking to a well-trained receptionist.

Sofia gracefully transfers the call to human staff with complete conversation context, so the caller never has to repeat themselves. If no staff is available, Sofia takes a detailed message including the caller's name, contact information, and request, then sends it via email or SMS to the appropriate team member.

Sofia is developed by Z.E Digital Tech (zedigital.tech), a technology company focused on AI solutions for businesses. The project is open-source and available on GitHub.

Technical

Security & Privacy

Yes. All data processing follows GDPR guidelines. Call data is processed in EU data centers, callers are informed about AI processing at the start of each call, and data is never retained longer than necessary. We provide a full Data Processing Agreement (DPA) upon request.

Call data is processed and stored in EU data centres (Hetzner). Call recordings (if enabled) are encrypted at rest and in transit. Data retention follows your configured policy, with automatic deletion after the retention period expires.

Call recording is optional and available on Professional and Enterprise plans. When enabled, callers are informed at the beginning of the call. Recordings are encrypted and can be accessed through the analytics dashboard. You can configure automatic deletion after a specified period.

Yes. All data is encrypted both in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). Voice data streams use secure WebSocket connections. We follow industry best practices for data security and regularly audit our security posture.

Sofia uses Zadarma for telephony (SIP trunking), LiveKit for real-time audio routing, Google Gemini Live for speech-to-speech AI processing, and Hetzner for hosting on EU servers. All services are selected for enterprise-grade security and GDPR compliance.
