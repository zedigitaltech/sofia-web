Built for Business, Powered by AI

Every feature designed to help businesses capture more leads, reduce costs, and deliver exceptional caller experiences.

Multilingual Support

Fluent in Albanian, English, Italian, German, French, and Spanish — six languages. Sofia automatically detects the caller's language and responds accordingly. Perfect for businesses serving diverse international communities.

24/7 Availability

Never miss a call again. Sofia operates 365 days a year, including holidays and peak seasons. No sick days, no vacation, no overtime. Consistent service at 3 AM and 3 PM alike.

Sub-300ms Latency

Ultra-fast response times powered by Hetzner EU infrastructure and Gemini Live's native audio pipeline. Callers experience natural conversation flow with no awkward pauses. Typical response latency under 500ms from speech detection to spoken reply.

Lead Qualification

Captures caller intent, determines their needs, and qualifies leads. Important calls are flagged and transferred to staff with full conversation context.

Custom Knowledge Base

Upload your business's specific information: services, pricing, policies, FAQs, team bios, and offerings. Changes take effect within minutes.

Analytics Dashboard

Track call volume, peak hours, common questions, lead conversion rates, and caller satisfaction. Actionable insights help you optimize your business operations and marketing.

SIP Telephony Integration

Seamless SIP trunk integration with your existing phone provider. No hardware changes needed at your location. Your existing phone number forwards to Sofia. Setup takes less than 24 hours.

LiveKit Video

Real-time audio streaming powered by LiveKit. Provides crystal-clear voice quality with adaptive bitrate for consistent performance even on unstable connections.

GDPR Compliance

Fully GDPR compliant. Data processed in EU data centers. Callers are informed about AI processing at the start of each call. We provide full DPA documentation upon request.

Call Recording

Optional call recording for quality assurance and training. Full transcripts available in the dashboard. Recordings are encrypted and stored securely per GDPR requirements.

Smart Routing

Intelligent call routing based on caller intent. Lead inquiries, complaints, and informational calls are handled differently. Complex requests are seamlessly transferred to human staff.

CRM Integration

Connect Sofia to your existing business management system. Caller data, lead details, and conversation logs sync automatically. Available on Professional and Enterprise plans.
